FAQ

What is the duration of the internship?
The internship typically lasts 1-3 months, flexible based on the program and your availability.
Do I get a certificate?
Yes, all successful participants receive a digital certificate verifiable online.
Who is eligible to apply?
Students from any technical background or those passionate about tech are welcome.
Is the internship remote?
Yes, all programs are 100% remote, allowing flexibility from anywhere.
Are there any fees?
No, all internships are completely free of charge.
How many hours per week?
Expect 10-20 hours per week, depending on the project.
